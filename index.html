About the Musician

Pobitra Kundu is a passionate professional musician with more than 15 years of experience in music and 5 years of teaching experience. He is mainly known as a sitarist and has performed in various prestigious musical events and competitions.

His musical journey started under the guidance of his first guru and father Prasanta Kundu. Later he trained under Niladri Kumar and currently continues his musical learning under his main mentor Dr Rajib Chakraborty. He is also a student at Rabindra Bharati University in West Bengal.
